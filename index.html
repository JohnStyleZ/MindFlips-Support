Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new deck?

Tap the "+" button in the Decks view, then select "Add Deck". Enter a name for your deck and tap "Create".

How do I add cards to my deck?

Navigate to a deck, then tap the "+" button in the top-right corner. You can also use the AI import feature to quickly create cards from various sources.

What is the difference between free and premium features?

The free version gives you access to basic flashcard creation and study features. Premium unlocks advanced features like AI-powered card generation, unlimited decks, progress tracking, and more.

How do I restore my premium purchase?

Go to Settings, scroll down to "Premium Features", and tap "Restore Purchases".

How do I organize my decks into groups?

In the Home view, tap the "+" button and select "Add Deck Group". Then you can move decks into groups by using the context menu (long press on a deck).

Can I import content from PDFs or images?

Yes, with the premium version. Go to the "Scan & AI" tab and select your desired import source.

How do I track my study progress?

Your study progress is displayed on the Home screen, showing stats for each deck as well as overall learning metrics.
