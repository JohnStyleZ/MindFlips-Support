About the App

MindFlip is an intuitive flashcard app that helps you study smarter. Swipe through cards, organize decks, and track your progress — all with a clean, focused interface. Built for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Silicon Macs.

How It Works

  • Tap a deck to start reviewing
  • Swipe right if you know the answer
  • Swipe left if you want to revisit later
  • Track your progress on the home screen
  • Works offline — no account needed

FAQ

Do I need an internet connection?
No, MindFlip works offline once installed.

Can I import from PDF or images?
Yes — just tap the “Scan” tab to get started.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Email us at support@mindflip.app
